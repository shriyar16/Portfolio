About Me




Currently, I am a freshman in high school. But, after I graduate, I would like to pursue a career in computer science engineering. To achieve this goal, I hope to gain exposure and develop an interest in this field throughout my four years of high school. I plan to join clubs and organizations relating to the engineering field and maybe start a club of my own to share what I have learned! Although I am fairly new to this area, I am excited to learn more and expand my learning. I believe this career is well suited for me because I enjoy working with computers and find the process fun and exciting. Also, I would like to make a change in the world and help people. A career in an industry like computer science seems to be a good way to achieve that.

Some of my major accomplishments from middle school include winning 5th place at the Bay Area Regional Science Olympiad tournament. I was on the same team and participated in the same events (Forensics, Epidemiology, and Optics) for two years. For the first year of the competition, my team and I did not do as well as we had hoped. So, in preparation for the following year, we worked more and studied harder. As a result, we placed 6th out of 30 schools across the Bay Area. I had also won 5th and 6th place in two of my events. Also, to gain more experience in the Computer Science field, I began to participate in Cyberpatriot. In my opinion, simply having the courage to try something new and being open to new ideas was an accomplishment. I was able to attend my first competition this past November. Although there were a few things I needed to improve on, I was proud of myself for being able to attend the competition and do my best.

My experiences throughout my schooling has taught me many valuable lessons. First, I had learned how to be patient with myself when taking a difficult class. Although academics are my strength, I got frustrated a lot when I did not understand something. This happened a lot during my Geometry class over the summer. Overtime, I learned to be patient with myself and give myself time to understand the material. Furthermore, after entering high school this year, I was able to grasp how to effectively manage all my classes and extracurriculars. Unlike middle school, I had a lot more on my plate and had to concentrate on balancing everything. I learned to manage my time and focus on my priorities.

Lastly, I believe I am a reliable person. I have been part of a couple clubs and teams and I enjoy working with others. I feel that I can be trusted with work and that I can perform my best. I enjoy being around people and interacting with others, especially people I do not know a lot about. I love talking to others and helping them out in any way I can.

All in all, thank you for taking your time to read this section of my portfolio. I encourage you to explore the rest of my website. Thank you!
